About me

I'm a Black, trans software engineer currently based in San Antonio, TX. In the past, I've worked and volunteered around prison abolition and led workshops on queer & trans solidarity. I'm also a former academic with a focus on Black, queer studies.

My passion for software engineering comes from a desire to create technologies and tools that center and serve my communities.

I value a practice rooted in care, clear boundaries, and honesty so that our collaborative process allows us both to show up authentically.

Outside of development I love Japanese Role Playing Games, cats, manga, plants, and cozy aesthetics.
